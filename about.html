About us

Welcome to Chango Secondary School, where excellence meets opportunity, and dreams take flight. Since our establishment in 1976, we have been dedicated to nurturing young minds, fostering leadership qualities, and instilling a passion for lifelong learning. Our Mission At Chango Secondary School, our mission is clear: to provide a dynamic and inclusive learning environment that empowers students to reach their full potential academically, socially, and personally. We believe that every student has unique talents waiting to be discovered and developed, and we are committed to guiding them on their journey to success.

We believe that every student has unique talents waiting to be discovered and developed, and we are committed to guiding them on their journey to success.
Our Vision Our vision is to be a beacon of educational excellence, recognized locally and globally for our innovative teaching methods, holistic approach to education, and unwavering commitment to student success. We aspire to cultivate a community of lifelong learners who are equipped with the skills, knowledge, and values to thrive in a rapidly changing world.
Core Values - **Excellence:** We strive for excellence in everything we do, encouraging our students to aim high, set ambitious goals, and pursue their passions with determination and perseverance. - **Integrity:** We uphold the highest standards of honesty, integrity, and ethical behavior, fostering a culture of trust, respect, and accountability within our school community.
